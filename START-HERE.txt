BLACKBOX MILESTONE 3C TEST BUILD

1. Extract the entire ZIP to a normal folder.
2. Double-click Blackbox.App.exe.
3. Click Setup OBS and wait for the ready message.

The first setup downloads the official OBS Studio Windows x64 portable package,
verifies it, and stores it under your local Blackbox app-data folder. It may take
several minutes. Administrator access is not required.

After setup succeeds, use Start Recording and Stop. Recordings and the short setup
test recording are stored in your Videos\Blackbox folder.

CURRENT LIMITATION

Blackbox creates the isolated game and voice-chat audio sources, but does not yet
choose their executable automatically. That is part of the upcoming game-profile
work. The setup check currently validates OBS launch, recording, file output,
sources, filters, and audio-track layout.

Logs: %LOCALAPPDATA%\Blackbox\logs
